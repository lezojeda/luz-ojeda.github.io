reading

Just finished Rebecca, a gothic mystery novel by Daphne du Maurier. Really pays off with some nice plot twists. Came across it looking for gothic gente books and it was a enticing read. I’ve enjoyed Lovecraft and Poe when I was a teen but didn’t even consider many of their tales fell under the gothic genre as well. I’ve been increasingly engaging in gothic content of all types such as movies as well (and becoming obsessed with candles in my apartment 😅).
